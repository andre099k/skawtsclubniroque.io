Scouting is a worldwide movement that helps young people develop character, leadership, and life skills through outdoor adventures, teamwork, and service to others. Scouts learn to live by values such as honesty, responsibility, respect, and care for the environment. Through camping, hiking, community service, and skill-building activities, Scouts grow in confidence and independence while forming lasting friendships. The goal of Scouting is to prepare young individuals to be responsible citizens and leaders who make a positive impact on their communities and the world.
